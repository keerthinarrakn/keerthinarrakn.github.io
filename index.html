D3 Narrative Visualization Project

This narrative visualization story uses Super Store dataset. I have tried the drill down hybrid structure for this vizualization.

This vizualization has three scenes in the narrative story.

  • Scene 1 shows the Quantity of orders made under Furniture category
  • Scene 2 shows the Quantity of orders made under Office Supplies category
  • Scene 3 shows the Quantity of orders made under Technology category

Quantity of each Category over Days, Weeks, Months explained using d3. It helps in understanding which category has sold the most quantity. This will help the store to keep up the sales in the profitable category and improve sales in categories with low sales.

Parameters

  • Order Date - This visualization uses date as a parameter to display the area chart. Parameter values are passed in either from the filter or zoom events.
  • Range for quantity - Option to choose various range to see the quantity sold.
  • Quantity - Quantity sold for a given category and a selected date range.

Events

  • Zoom Out - Zoom out event, summarizes the data. For instance user can zoom out from April 2014 to year 2017 to view yearly sales instead of monthly sale trend.
  • Zoom In - Zoom in event, drills down the data. For instance user can zoom in from 2014 to a particular day to view daily sales.
  • Filter - Context filter is present below the main chart. This filters data based on the date range provided by the user.
  • Filter Move - This event moves the fixed data range up or down by sliding the context filter.

Triggers

  • Zoom & Brush Trigger - Mouse drag inside the chart or adjusting the date filter boundary in context filter will fire this trigger.
  • On mouse over Trigger - This trigger will show the tooltip data on mouse over.

Annotations

  • Tool Tip - This tooltip shows quantity where the mouse pointer is.
  • Axis Labels - Annotates what is the x-axis and y-axis for the chart.

Drill Down

  • This story uses zoom out and zoom in feature of D3 to drill down the data.

Future Improvements

  • I would like to further drill down and view the sub-categories as well.
